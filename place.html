About Kenya

Kenya is a country in East Africa with a beautiful blend of landscapes, wildlife, and culture. From the Great Rift Valley to the Indian Ocean coast, Kenya is famous for its safaris, game reserves, and vibrant traditions.

The capital city is Nairobi, a hub of culture and business, while Mombasa offers rich Swahili heritage and stunning beaches.
